General School Rules

Students must be punctual and attend all classes as per the timetable. Respect for teachers, staff, and fellow students is mandatory.

Academic Policies

All students must complete assignments on time. Plagiarism and cheating in exams will not be tolerated.

Code of Conduct

Bullying, harassment, and any form of discrimination are strictly prohibited. Proper attire must be worn at all times.

Technology Usage

Use of school computers and internet resources must be for educational purposes only. Cyberbullying and access to inappropriate content are forbidden.
